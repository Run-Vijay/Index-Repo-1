Welcome to Mohit's Portfolio | Projects

Over the past few years, I have worked on a variety of projects that showcase my skills in web development and problem-solving. My portfolio includes responsive websites, interactive web applications, and creative UI/UX designs. Some of my notable projects include a personal blog platform built with React and Node.js, an e-commerce website with a custom shopping cart, and a weather dashboard that fetches real-time data from APIs. Each project has helped me learn new technologies and improve my ability to deliver user-friendly and efficient solutions. I am always excited to take on new challenges and bring innovative ideas to life through code.
